Concepts

Responsible and ethical behavior-This includes being kind online, avoiding harassment and not sharing ir creating harmful content. It also means not engaging in Illegal activities like hacking or seating

Digital literacy- this includes the ability to find, evaluate and use information found online in a knowledgeable and legal way. It also involves understanding concepts like copyright and creative credit.

Concepts

Safety and security-This involves protecting your personal information from threats like identity theft and phishing, managing passwords and recognizing the risks of oversharing.

Community and respect-Just as in the offline world, digital citizenship involves participating in online communities with respect for others and using technology to make a positive impact.
